About Frog Blog

Well, I think firstly the reason I created this blog is because the word blog rhymes with the word frog. I am very creative. Secondarily, I've always been fascinated by amphibians and I thought that writing about a few transformative experiences related to this group of extraordinary creatures might be an interesting meditative exercise. So, what began as a very silly (and basic) free associative, nonsensical rhyme led to something I actually had an interest in doing. Pretty neat, huh? No? Yeah I know.
